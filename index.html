INTO THE RABBITHOLE

In 1951 a enormous rabbithole merge out the center of the earth. Those who survive the rabbithole didn’t know what the hole have plans for them. But to their surprise, the rabbithole is a world replica of a book called “Alice in Wonderland”. This mystery carry on for over 60 years, each year there will be new victim fell into the hole. No one knows the reason why the sudden disaster happened in that era. But now all they know is to get out and survive the DISASTER itself ‘the Rabbit’.
